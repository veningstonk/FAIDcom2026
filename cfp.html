Call for Papers

FAIDcom 2025 invites original research contributions in the following technical tracks. Accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore.

Technical Tracks

Track 1: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Explainable AI
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • AI in Healthcare and Finance
  • Edge AI and Federated Learning

Track 2: Data Science and Big Data Analytics

  • Big Data Processing and Analytics
  • Data Visualization and Interpretation
  • Predictive Modeling and Forecasting
  • Data Ethics and Privacy
  • Blockchain in Data Security

Track 3: Cloud Computing and IoT

  • Edge and Fog Computing
  • IoT-based Smart Applications
  • Cybersecurity in IoT
  • Cloud-native Architectures
  • 5G and Beyond Technologies

Submission Guidelines

All submitted papers must follow the IEEE Conference Proceedings format and be written in English. Submissions should be in PDF format and not exceed 6 pages, including figures and references.

Paper Format:

  • Use IEEE double-column format.
  • Manuscripts should be original and not published elsewhere.
  • Plagiarism should be below the acceptable IEEE threshold.
  • Submissions should be made through the official conference submission portal.

For detailed submission instructions, please visit the Paper Submission page.
