About NIT Srinagar

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Srinagar is one of the premier engineering institutions in India. Established in 1960, NIT Srinagar has a long tradition of excellence in education, research, and innovation. It is an autonomous institution under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. NIT Srinagar offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering and science disciplines.

The institute is known for its commitment to academic rigor, cutting-edge research, and fostering a vibrant student community. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and world-class faculty, NIT Srinagar continues to shape the future of technological advancements in India and beyond.

About KGiSL

KGiSL (KG Information Systems Private Limited) is a leading global provider of IT solutions and services. With a strong presence in software development, data analytics, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing, KGiSL has been at the forefront of technological innovations.

Headquartered in India, KGiSL has a vast global reach and collaborates with industry leaders to deliver digital transformation solutions. The organization is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and driving progress in AI and data computing.
